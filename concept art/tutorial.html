How to begin?

If you want to make good concept art, you need to evoke a specific mood in the observer. The mood depends on the kind of painting you set out to make.

Bakcground tone

First of all set a bacgrkound tone for your painting. It helps you create a mood. You can make a grid for a better composition. With a pencil or charcoal sketch the desired outcome.

Color blocking

Next step is to block out the colors of objects in the image. Don't worry you don't need to paint the sketch exactly, you can play around with shapes until you like what you see.

Shading

Now you add the shading. Define the shapes and add texture.

Adding details

Finish off with details, like painting some trees or birds, maybe a bit of fog. You can adjust levels for a better effect. And, YOUR DONE!
